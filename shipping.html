Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

Delivery Areas

Sastosaman currently delivers to all major cities and districts in Nepal. If you're unsure whether we deliver to your area, please contact our customer service team for confirmation.

Shipping Methods & Timeframes

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping (Kathmandu Valley) 2-4 business days NRs 150
Express Shipping (Kathmandu Valley) 1-2 business days NRs 300
Standard Shipping (Outside Valley) 5-7 business days NRs 200-400
Express Shipping (Outside Valley) 3-5 business days NRs 400-600

Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary depending on your location and other factors such as weather conditions or local events.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive an email notification once your order has been processed and shipped, along with tracking information where available.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number to track your order on our website or contact our customer service team for updates.

Shipping Restrictions

Some remote areas may have limited delivery options or additional delivery charges. We'll contact you if there are any issues with shipping to your location.

Returns Policy

Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if:

  • The item doesn't match the description or has undisclosed defects
  • You received an incorrect item
  • The item was damaged during shipping

To be eligible for a return, the item must be in the same condition as when it was shipped, with all tags and packaging intact. Items that have been worn, washed, or altered are not eligible for return.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order to request a return.
  2. Our team will provide you with a return authorization and instructions.
  3. Package the item securely in its original packaging if possible.
  4. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping method.
  5. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund or exchange.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer unless the return is due to our error (such as sending the wrong item or a damaged product). In such cases, we will reimburse your return shipping costs.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.

  • For cash on delivery orders, refunds will be processed to your bank account (you'll need to provide your bank details).
  • For eSewa or Khalti payments, refunds will be processed back to your eSewa or Khalti account.

Exchanges

If you'd prefer to exchange an item rather than return it, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the item you want is not available, we'll process a refund instead.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned due to hygiene and safety reasons, including:

  • Underwear and intimate apparel
  • Swimwear
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
