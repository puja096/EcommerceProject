Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with Sastosaman.

What is Sastosaman?

Sastosaman is a second-hand clothing store based in Nepal that offers quality pre-loved clothing at affordable prices. We're committed to promoting sustainable fashion by giving clothes a second life and reducing textile waste.

How does Sastosaman source its clothing?

We source our clothing through various channels, including direct purchases from individuals, partnerships with clothing collectors, and carefully selected imports. All items undergo a thorough quality check and cleaning process before being listed for sale.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, we have a physical store located in Kathmandu at 123 Fashion Street. Our store hours are Monday to Friday from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Saturday from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and we're closed on Sundays.

Can I sell my clothes to Sastosaman?

Yes, we do accept clothing from individuals. Please visit our physical store with the items you'd like to sell. Our team will assess the condition, brand, and current demand for the items to determine if we can accept them and what we can offer for them.

How do you determine the condition of clothing?

We use a four-tier condition rating system:

  • Like New: Items appear unworn or unused, often with original tags/packaging.
  • Excellent: Items show minimal signs of wear and have been well-maintained.
  • Good: Items show some signs of wear consistent with regular use, but no major flaws.
  • Fair: Items show noticeable wear and may have minor flaws, but remain functional and wearable.

Are the clothes cleaned before being sold?

Yes, all clothing items undergo a thorough cleaning process before being listed for sale. Depending on the material and condition, items are either professionally dry-cleaned or washed according to their care instructions.

How often do you add new items?

We add new items to our inventory several times a week, both online and in our physical store. For the latest additions, check the "New Arrivals" section on our website or follow us on social media for updates.

Do you sell anything other than clothing?

Currently, we focus primarily on clothing items. However, we occasionally offer accessories such as bags, scarves, and jewelry. We're considering expanding our product range in the future based on customer demand.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver throughout Nepal. Delivery times and costs vary depending on your location. We offer standard delivery (3-5 business days) and express delivery (1-2 business days) options.

How much does shipping cost?

Standard shipping within Kathmandu Valley costs NRs 150, while express shipping costs NRs 300. For locations outside the valley, standard shipping ranges from NRs 200-400 depending on the distance and accessibility.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number to track your order on our website or contact our customer service team for updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • eSewa
  • Khalti
  • Cash or card payment at our physical store

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the item doesn't match the description or has undisclosed defects. The item must be in the same condition as when it was shipped, with all tags and packaging intact.

How do I return an item?

To return an item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order. They will guide you through the return process and provide a return authorization. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer unless the return is due to our error.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method and financial institution.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, we do offer exchanges if you'd prefer a different size or item. Please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order to arrange an exchange. Note that exchanges are subject to product availability.

Do I need to create an account to shop?

While you can browse our products without an account, creating an account is required to make a purchase. Having an account allows you to track orders, save favorite items, and enjoy a smoother checkout process for future purchases.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your payment information. We do not store your complete payment details on our servers.

How can I change my account information?

You can update your account information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Account Settings" or "Profile" section. From there, you can modify your personal details, shipping addresses, and communication preferences.

What if I forgot my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll be prompted to enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.
