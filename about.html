About Sastosaman

Our Story

Sastosaman was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: to make quality clothing accessible to everyone while promoting sustainable fashion practices. What started as a small collection of pre-loved items has grown into a curated marketplace of second-hand clothing treasures.

Our founder, Aarav Sharma, started Sastosaman after noticing the growing problem of textile waste in Nepal. With a background in retail and a passion for sustainability, Aarav set out to create a business that would have a positive impact on both people and the planet.

Our Mission

At Sastosaman, we believe that fashion shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. By giving clothes a second life, we're reducing waste and the environmental impact of the fashion industry. Our mission is to make sustainable fashion choices accessible, affordable, and exciting for everyone.

We're committed to promoting a circular economy in fashion, where clothing items are reused, recycled, and valued for their quality and craftsmanship rather than their newness.
